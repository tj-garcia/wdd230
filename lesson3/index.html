Typography

Heading Font: Merriweather

Merriweather was designed to be pleasant to read on screens, elegant, and representative for business projects without lost the seriousness of the design. In titles and subtitles, it looks great and catches the reader's attention easily to a specific area in the site.

Paragraph Font: Lato

Lato is a san-serif typeface very clean and elegant, not too distracting, with a touch of warmth. It is highly used on websites to deliver information directly. The use of this particular font could make information appears to be simple, neat, and delivers important information straight away to its receivers.
